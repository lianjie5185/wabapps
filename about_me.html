Two years ago, I immigrated from China to the United States. I am very fortunate to be able to study in such a strong education in the United States.

But this is not simple. Language has become my biggest obstacle.

As far as I know, many new immigrants, not only students, have difficulties in language communicate.

I noticed that there are many free educational resources on the web that can be used.

If you can make good use of these resources, it will be a great help for those who really want to learn.

That is my initial idea of making this website.
